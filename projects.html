My Recent Project

My most recent project was the Environmental Quiz I made on Python. It consisted of 10 questions based on topics based around the environment. These topics consisted of: Climate Change, Energy, Recycling, Carbon Emissions & Pollution. The original outline for the code was for it to have a working scoreboard at the end of it. However due to time constraints, I didn't add the scoreboard. But overall it works very well, just wish that I could've added more features to it.
